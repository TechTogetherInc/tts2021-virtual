Tracks / Challenges

Best Beginner Hack


Simply beginning is one of the hardest barriers to overcome. We are awarding the hack that demonstrates the most growth in relation to the hackers' knowledge coming into TechTogether Seattle. We are looking for reflection in the hacker’s submission and how you overcame these challenges.

Best Meme-Worthy Hack


It’s totally okay to let loose once in a while. We want to recognize the hacker who created the most wacky hack. The more roundabout and out-right silly – the better.

Inclusive Hack


Learning to Code should not be limited by gender, race, sexuality, or nationality. Build a solution that creates greater access to computer science for students around the globe, especially gender-marginalized people and those from marginalized communities.
